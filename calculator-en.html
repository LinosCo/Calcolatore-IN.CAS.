Total safety every day

S.P.R.I.Z. has been designed and built to allow healthcare professionals to manage organic fluids in complete safety, in compliance with legal regulations.

The complete traceability of all fundamental functions of the aspirator guarantees a new safety standard.

BIOLOGICAL CONTAMINATION RISK: eliminates the risk of contact with potentially contaminated biological fluids.
LIFTING AND MOVING: avoids continuous lifting and moving by operators of fluid collection bags.
ASPIRATED FLUID DISINFECTION: in infected biological fluid aspiration mode, activates a real-time disinfection system with precision peristaltic pump for correct disinfectant dosing.
EQUIPMENT AUTO-DISINFECTION: high level of aspirator auto-disinfection to ensure maximum safety.
